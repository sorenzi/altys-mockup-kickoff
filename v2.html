Lesson kickoff — postcards

Tall vertical cards with a colored art canvas on top and a white info strip below — each topic feels like a collectible postcard. Three viewports below.

What changed from V1

  • 2 columns everywhere. Phone, iPad portrait, iPad landscape (in the left column). No more 3-col anywhere.
  • Cards are tall postcards now — pastel art canvas on top with a big emoji, white info strip below with topic name (Fraunces serif) and a one-line teaser. No more squat list rows.
  • iPad landscape is packed. Helper card removed from the left (redundant with the right panel). All 8 topics fit in the left column. Right panel fills the column edge to edge: poster top, content body, sticky CTA pinned at the bottom.
