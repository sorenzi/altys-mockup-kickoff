ALT-732 · Cover Flow Carousel · Concept · 2026-05-04

Hero Stage v3 — cover flow (focal scaling)

Discrete cards instead of full-bleed gradients. Center card is largest and fully colored; neighbors scale to ~62% and fade to ~55% opacity, peeking from the canvas edges. Canvas background stays still during the swipe and snaps to a soft tint of the focal character's palette only after the swipe commits — no jarring full-bleed transitions.

  • Apple's name for this: Cover Flow (iTunes / iPod / Photos). Same conceptual model: focused item is largest and centered; siblings scale and fade outward.
  • Lore panel stays exactly as-is — bottom sheet on compact, right column on landscape. Only the canvas changes.
  • Discovery: the second character is visible the moment the screen renders, no chip needed.
  • "Meet Kai" chip removed — the visible peek card replaces it. Sidesteps the chip-vs-CTA adjacency confusion entirely.
  • Background tint snaps on swipe commit — Luma focal → soft pink-cream; Kai focal → soft blue-cream. Subtle enough to not compete with the cards.
